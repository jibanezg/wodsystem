Attribute Guide

  • Physical: Strength, Dexterity, Stamina - your body's capabilities
  • Social: Charisma, Manipulation, Appearance - your social prowess
  • Mental: Perception, Intelligence, Wits - your mind's abilities
  • All attributes start at 1. You cannot reduce them below 1.
  • You must spend ALL points in each category before continuing.
